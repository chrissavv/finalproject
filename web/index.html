What is a meme? According to Dictionary.com, a meme is “a humorous image, video, piece of text, etc., that is copied (often with slight variations) and spread rapidly by Internet users.” Yet to many of us who have laughed at one or shaken our heads in silent empathy with another, a meme is so much more.
It is a source of gentle humor after a long day at the lab or in the library and a way to encompass our never-ending list of complaints about being students in one image and a few lines of text.
